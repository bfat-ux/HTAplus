# HTA Document Template — Staff Guide

## What's in the template

The **HTA_Document_Template.docx** is a ready-to-use starting point for training inquiries, management reports, and general correspondence. It includes:

- **Branded header** with the HTA name, tagline, and a placeholder for your logo.
- **Metadata fields** for Date, Prepared By, Department, Subject, and Reference Number.
- **Service Category selector** (Training / Management) so every document is properly classified.
- **Six standard body sections**: Executive Summary, Background, Scope & Objectives, Detailed Findings, Recommendations, and Appendices.
- **Approval sign-off table** with spaces for Prepared By, Reviewed By, and Approved By.
- **Branded footer** with the Lagos office address, htaplus.com, and page numbers.

## How to save it as a Google Docs template

1. Open Google Drive and navigate to the folder where you want to keep the template.
2. Upload **HTA_Document_Template.docx** (drag-and-drop or use the **+ New > File upload** button).
3. Double-click the uploaded file to open it in Google Docs.
4. At the top, click **File > Save as Google Docs**. This converts it to native Google Docs format.
5. Go to **docs.google.com** and click **Template gallery** (top-right area).
6. Click **Submit template**, select the Google Docs version you just created, and give it a name like "HTA Official Template".
7. Choose the appropriate category (e.g., "Work") and click **Submit**.

Your team can now start new documents from this template directly from the Template gallery.

> **Note:** The Template gallery is available on Google Workspace Business, Enterprise, and Education plans. If you don't see it, ask your Workspace admin to enable it under **Admin Console > Apps > Google Workspace > Drive and Docs > Templates**.

## Alternative: Use as a Google Docs copy-from-master

If your plan doesn't include the Template gallery:

1. Upload and convert the file as described in steps 1–4 above.
2. Rename it to something clear, like "HTA Master Template — DO NOT EDIT".
3. When staff need a new document, they open the master and go to **File > Make a copy**, then rename and work from the copy.

## Formatting tips for staff

- Replace all placeholder text in **[square brackets]** with your actual content.
- To insert the HTA logo, click the "[ Insert HTA Logo Here ]" text, delete it, and use **Insert > Image**.
- Use **Heading 1** for main sections and **Heading 2** for sub-sections to keep the document navigable.
- Tick the appropriate Service Category box by replacing the checkbox character with a filled one, or simply bold your selection.
